Academics

Courses & Academics

NMC-approved programs designed to build confident, skilled and compassionate medical professionals.

MBBS

HIMS offers an NMC-approved MBBS degree affiliated with MGKVP University. Students train from Day 1 at our own 1,000-bed hospital — one of the largest in Purvanchal — gaining real clinical exposure across all major departments.

  • Live OPD & ward rotations from Year 1
  • Faculty from AIIMS, BHU, KGMU & CMC
  • State Govt. fee policy — no hidden charges
4.5 Yrs + 1 Yr Internship Undergraduate
Know more

PG Courses

HIMS postgraduate programs — MD, MS and Diploma — are designed for doctors ready to specialize. With access to a high-volume hospital, regular CME sessions and experienced senior faculty, HIMS is where specialists are shaped.

  • Specializations in Medicine, Surgery & Radiology
  • Active research support & academic publications
  • High case load for deep clinical specialization
3 Years Postgraduate
Know more

Nursing

HIMS Nursing programs — B.Sc and M.Sc — are affiliated with ABVM University and follow an NMC-approved curriculum. Students train directly inside our 1,000-bed hospital across ICU, OT, pediatrics and emergency care units.

  • Hands-on ICU, OT & Emergency ward training
  • Dedicated & secure hostel facility on campus
  • Strong placement support after completion
B.Sc / M.Sc NMC Approved
Know more

Allied Health

HIMS Allied Health programs train the next generation of paramedical professionals. From Lab Technology and Physiotherapy to Radiology — students graduate with industry-ready skills and immediate employability in modern healthcare.

  • Streams: Lab Tech, Physio, Radiology & more
  • Live lab training inside HIMS hospital facilities
  • High employment rate after Diploma & B.Sc
Diploma / B.Sc Job-Ready
